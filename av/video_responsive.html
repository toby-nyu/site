Video Example

This page demonstrates responsive video that adapts to your screen size

JavaScript automatically selects the appropriate video resolution based on your window width:

  • 1920px and wider: 1080p HD video
  • 1280px - 1919px: 720p HD video
  • Below 1280px: 480p SD video

The video switches resolution when you resize the browser window (with a 250ms debounce delay)

The video is muted, autoplays, loops, and fits the full width of the page

On mobile devices, the playsinline attribute prevents fullscreen playback
